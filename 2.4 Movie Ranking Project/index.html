The Best Movies of All Time

My top 3 movies of all time

The Nice Guys (2016)

Ryan Gosling and Russell Crowe; a very dry, humourous action comedy

Interstellar (2014)

Mathew McConaughey and Jessica Chastain; a mind bending sci-fi that is shot amazingly

Sinners (2025)

Michael B. Jordan and Hailee Steinfield; a masterpiece that combines music, action and historical accuracy
